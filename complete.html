Our Story

Threads & Finds was born from a passion for sustainable fashion and the thrill of discovering unique vintage pieces. We believe that great style doesn't have to come at the expense of our planet.

Our team scours thrift stores, estate sales, and vintage markets to bring you carefully curated clothing with character and history. Each piece is inspected, cleaned, and photographed with care.

By choosing secondhand, you're not just getting unique fashion - you're reducing textile waste and supporting a more sustainable future for fashion.
